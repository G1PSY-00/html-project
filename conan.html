Detective Conan


Status: Ongoing

Episodes: 1000+

Aired: January 8, 1996 to ongoing

Genres: Mystery, Shonen manga, Thriller, Detective/Mystery

Synopsis: Shinichi Kudo is a teenage detective who is ambushed and given a dangerous experimental drug by the Black Organization. The drug shrinks his body to that of a child, but he retains his intelligence. To hide his true identity, he takes the name Conan Edogawa and lives with his childhood friend Ran Mouri and her father, Kogoro Mouri, a private detective. Conan enrolls in elementary school and joins the Junior Detective League, while also helping Kogoro on his cases.
