Comedy Anime Recommendations

Bocchi the Rock

Follow the story of Hitori Gotou, a painfully shy girl with a passion for the guitar, who unexpectedly finds herself as part of a rock band. A mix of relatable social struggles and humorous band antics, this anime is both endearing and hilarious.

Grand Blue Dreaming

Iori Kitahara's college life begins with unexpected debauchery as he joins a diving club full of eccentric characters. A combination of outlandish comedy and friendship, this series shows college life like no other.

More info

Great Teacher Onizuka

Ex-biker gang leader Eikichi Onizuka becomes a teacher, determined to bring out the best in his troubled students with unconventional and hilarious teaching methods. A mix of heartwarming moments and outrageous humor.

More info

K-On!

Join Yui Hirasawa and her friends as they form a high school music club that’s more about tea and sweets than actual practice. With plenty of lighthearted moments, this anime celebrates friendship and the joy of making music together.

More info

KonoSuba

After an untimely death, Kazuma Satou finds himself in a fantasy world, only to realize that his new life isn’t as glamorous as he expected. With a dysfunctional party, he embarks on comedic adventures filled with hilarious misfortunes.

More info

Mob Psycho 100

Shigeo “Mob” Kageyama, a powerful psychic, wants nothing more than to lead a normal life. Under the mentorship of a less-than-genuine psychic, he navigates adolescence and learns to control his overwhelming powers in this blend of action and comedy.

More info

Prison School

Five boys enroll in an all-girls school and find themselves in constant trouble. Hilarious and raunchy, this anime chronicles their misadventures as they try to survive strict punishment from the underground student council.

More info

Kill la Kill

In a world where clothing holds power, Ryuko Matoi, armed with a powerful weapon, seeks revenge for her father’s death. Mixing over-the-top action with wild humor, this anime is an energetic journey filled with suspense and laughs.
