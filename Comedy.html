Bocchi the Rock

Follow the story of Hitori Gotou, a painfully shy girl with a passion for the guitar, who unexpectedly finds herself as part of a rock band. A mix of relatable social struggles and humorous band antics, this anime is both endearing and hilarious.

Grand Blue Dreaming

Iori Kitahara's college life begins with unexpected debauchery as he joins a diving club full of eccentric characters. A combination of outlandish comedy and friendship, this series shows college life like no other.

Great Teacher Onizuka

Ex-biker gang leader Eikichi Onizuka becomes a teacher, determined to bring out the best in his troubled students with unconventional and hilarious teaching methods. A mix of heartwarming moments and outrageous humor.

K-On!

Join Yui Hirasawa and her friends as they form a high school music club that’s more about tea and sweets than actual practice. With plenty of lighthearted moments, this anime celebrates friendship and the joy of making music together.

KonoSuba

Trapped in a fantasy world with a goddess and a group of quirky companions, Kazuma Satou’s life goes from ordinary to absurdly hilarious. Watch as they navigate absurd quests and situations, bringing comedy in every moment.

Mob Psycho 100

Shigeo "Mob" Kageyama is a seemingly ordinary middle schooler with powerful psychic abilities. Watch as he struggles with normal life and the wacky situations his powers create in this laugh-out-loud, action-packed comedy.

Prison School

Five high school boys are sent to a strict all-girls school and forced to serve time in the prison-like detention facility after they’re caught peeping. This outrageous and often raunchy comedy pushes boundaries in the most absurd ways.

Kill la Kill

Ryuko Matoi seeks revenge for her father’s death while battling a school ruled by powerful students wearing special outfits. This fast-paced series blends action with humor and an over-the-top art style.
